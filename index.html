Background

I have completed my BSc. in Mechanical Engineering (Robotics Major) from BUET and opting for an MSc./PhD degree in Computer Science or related field. I am currently working full-time as a Graduate Research Assistant at Model and Project Lab in BUET and part-time as the STEM division head at Youthprenuer Network in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I have previous experience of participating in International Space related Robotics Competition (European Rover Challenge - 2019) as a Software Team Lead in the year 2019. Recently, I am serving as the Software team advisor for Team Interplanetar - 2020 with 3.5 years of working experience in the previous team (Team Interplanetar - 2017, 2018 & 2019).

Currently, my research works are focused (but not limited) towards applying Deep Learning in Computer Vision for Autonomous Navigation, Deep Reinforcement Learning in Game Theory for Practical Robotics Application and Computer Vision, Machine Learning & Cloud Computing in Data Science for dealing with Astronomy at Grand scale.

My work will serve the following purposes:
● Ensuring reliable autonomous navigation for Aerial (or Marine) vehicles to distribute flood reliefs in hostile environments with complex vision processing and better understand human psychology during flood relief collection.
● Build better navigation system for humanoid robots & rovers to facilitate safe exploration for humans on ground, deep inside mineral ores and on the surface of oceans.
● Studying stars, galaxies and black holes with Computer Vision, Big Data & Machine Learning in order to solve fundamental yet challenging problems of Modern Astronomy.
