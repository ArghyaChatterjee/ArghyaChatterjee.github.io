Background

I have completed my BSc. in Mechanical Engineering (Robotics Major) from BUET and opting for an MSc./PhD degree in Computer Science or related field. I am currently working part time as a Graduate Research Assistant at Models and Projects Lab in BUET and full-time as the STEM division head at Youthprenuer Network in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I have previous experience of participating in International Space related Robotics Competition (European Rover Challenge - 2019) as a Software Team Lead in the year 2019. Recently, I am serving as the Software team advisor for Team Interplanetar - 2020 with 3.5 years of working experience in the previous team (Team Interplanetar - 2017, 2018 & 2019).

Currently, my research works are focused (but not limited) towards applying Deep Learning in Computer Vision for Autonomous Navigation, Deep Reinforcement Learning in Game Theory for Practical Robotics Application and Machine Learning & Cloud Computing in Data Science for dealing with Astronomy at Grand scale.

My work will serve the following purposes:
● Ensure reliable autonomous navigation for Marine & Aerial Vehicles to provide secure monitoring and surveillance for humans and marine habitants on earth.
● Build better navigation system for humanoid robots & rovers to facilitate safe exploration for humans on ground, deep inside mineral ores and on the surface of oceans.
● Lead investigation on cosmology one step forward by studying the behaviour of stars, galaxies and black holes with Big Data & Intelligent Computing in order to solve fundamental yet challenging problems of Modern Astronomy.
